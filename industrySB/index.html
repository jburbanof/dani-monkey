Barbie Spring-break is one new adventure for Barbie and the first time I was able to work as a Storyboard Artist in a short-term production, I had a lot of fun working with the director on this sequence.

Care-bears was a production made in Copernicus, I was part of the team as a Storyboard Revisionist, and this is a little showcase of one of the sequence that we worked together.
